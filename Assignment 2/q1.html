Image of Enger Tower

Enger tower is one beutiful place. It has a American-Japanese style park at the base of the tower. The park features a Peace bell that was donated from the sister city of Ohara-Isumi. At the top of the tower you get one of the best views of duluth, you can see the following locations from here, Great Lakes Aquarium, Park Point, Lester river, the aerial lift bridge and much more.

Image of Gooseberry falls

This is gooseberry falls. This one is in twin harbors so it is a little drive north from duluth. This location is worth the drive. Whether it is summer or winter you are sure to get some spectatular sights. If you go in the summer, you can also swim there too, however the water is just as cold as lake superior so even in summer it is a chilly dip. In the winter, you obviously don't get to swim but sometimes the waterfall will freeze over and you will get this beutiful sight of a waterfall frozen in time.

Image of Great Lakes Aquarium

A fun place to visit at least once is the Great Lakes Aquarium. This is a place that I have visited multiple times growing up. While it has some fun This aquarium houses all sorts of animals from the Great Lakes, the aquarium also lets you pet the stingrays. They are also known for their informational exhibits too, they have on interactive one where you can take a toy ship through the multiple locks of the great lakes.

Image of Hawk Ridge

Hawk Ridge is a recent place that I have visited. This is another spot that is great for the views. Along the way up there you were able to get multiple views of lakes superior and the bay, which were just stunning. When you get to the top you will reach an observation platform where you can look for birds. The birds that you can see are listed nearby by season so you can plan another visit during a time you want to see a certain bird.
